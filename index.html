Welcome to HelpFromAfar

As a self-serving business, we provide every means for migrant workers across states and regions in India to find the help they need and the services they require. By the combined efforts of professionals and volunteers alike, it's in our interest to promote the welfare of the non-native manual laborers and wage workers alike across the nation.

Legal Services

Our team of experienced legal professionals is dedicated to helping migrant workers understand their rights and resolve legal issues they might face while working in different regions of India.
